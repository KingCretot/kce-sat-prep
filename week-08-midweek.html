Q1Conventions
“Neither the manager nor the employees ______ aware of the change.”
Answer: B
With “nor,” the verb matches the nearer subject — “employees” (plural) → were.
Q2Conventions
Best correction of “I wanted to go, the weather was bad.”
Answer: A
A semicolon joins the two complete sentences correctly.
Q3Conventions
“Each of the girls wore ______ medal proudly.”
Answer: A
“Each” is singular, so the singular pronoun “her” agrees.
Q4Conventions
“That is my ______ book.” (the book belonging to one sister)
Answer: C
Singular possessive adds ’s → sister’s.
Q5Conventions
“She ______ in Paris for three years before she moved to Rome.”
Answer: B
The Paris years came first → past perfect “had lived.”
